  • 1. Q.What does AR 600-20 cover?

    • Army Command Policy.

  • 2. Q.What chapter covers the EO program in AR 600-20?

    • Chapter 6./p>

  • 3. Q.What does Ch 6 of AR 600-20 cover?

    • The Army’s EO Program.

  • 4. Q.Para 6-1 What is the purpose of the EO program?

    • To ensure fair treatment for all persons based solely on merit, fitness, and capability in support of readiness.

  • 5. Q.Para 6-1 What is the EO policy based on?

    • Fairness, justice, and equity.

  • 6. Q.Para 6-1 Who is responsible for maintaining a positive EO climate in the unit?

    • the commander.

  • 7. Q.Para 6-1 What are the goals of the EO program?

    • 1. Provide EO for military personnel and Family members, both on and off post. 2. Create and sustain effective units by eliminating discriminatory behaviors or practices that undermine teamwork.

  • 8. Q.Para 6-2 When does the Army’s EO policy apply?

    • 1. Applies both on and off post, during duty and non-duty hours. 2. Applies to working, living, and recreational environments.

  • 9. Q.Para 6-2 What is Discrimination?

    • Any action that unlawfully or unjustly results in unequal treatment of persons or groups based on race, color, gender, national origin, or religion.

  • 10. Q.Para 6-2 What is Disparaging terms?

    • Terms used to degrade or connote negative statements pertaining to race, color, gender, national origin, or religion.
