  • 1. What publication covers Leaves and Passes?

    • AR 600-8-10.

  • 2. What does AR 600-8-10 cover?

    • Leaves and Passes.

  • 3. Para 2-2 What Soldiers run the risk of losing leave?

    • Soldiers who maintain a 60-day leave balance, and wait late in the FY to take leave.

  • 4. Para 2-3 How much Leave do Soldiers on active duty earn?

    • Soldiers on active duty earn 30 days of leave a year at the rate of 2 1⁄2 days a month.

  • 5. Para 2-3 What situations do not allow Soldiers to earn leave?

    • (1) Soldiers AWOL. (2) Confinement. (3) Confinement. (4) Excess leave. (5) Unauthorized absence. (6) Absence in custody. (7) Absence over 1 duty day due to use of drugs or alcohol.

  • 6. Para 2-3 How many days of leave can Soldiers carry forward at beginning of fiscal year?

    • No more than 60.

  • 7. Para 2-3 What happens if a Soldier carries more than 60 days of leave on 1 Oct?

    • The Soldier will lose any days over 60.

  • 8. Para 2-4 What is the maximum amount of leave that a Soldier can be paid for over a career?

    • Payment of accrued leave is limited to 60 days one time during a military career.

  • ​9. Para 3-1 What is the intent of special leave accrual?

    • The intent of special leave accrual is to provide relief to Soldiers who are not allowed leave when undergoing lengthy deployment or during periods of hostility.

  • 10. Para 3-2 Who is authorized special leave accrual?

    • To Soldiers who served in an area in which he or she was entitled to hostile fire or imminent danger pay for at least 120 continuous days.
