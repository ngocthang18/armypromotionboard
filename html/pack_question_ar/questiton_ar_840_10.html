  • 1. What publication covers Flags and Guidons?

    • AR 840-10.

  • 2. What does AR 840-10 cover?

    • Flags and Guidons.

  • 3. Para 1-4 What does TIOH stand for?

    • The Institute of Heraldry.

  • 4. Para 1-4 What is TIOH responsible for?

    • (1) Developing and approving policies and procedures for the Army flag program. (2) Monitoring the overall operation of the Army flag program.

  • 5. Para 1-4 Who is responsible for the care and storage of flags of inactive TOE units?

    • U.S. Army Center of Military History.

  • 6. Para 1-7 What a flags, guidons, streamers, and components are prohibited?

    • hose that are not listed in AR 840-10 or approved by the TIOH.

  • 7. Para 1-8 To get automatically promoted what must be met?

    • 1. Time in Service (TIS). 2. Time in Grade (TIMIG). 3. Unit Commander must Authorize the Promotion

  • 8. Para 1-8 What two ranks may be placed on the automatic promotion integration list?

    • SPC and SGT.

  • ​​​​​9. Para 1-9 Who is the promotion authority for promotions PV2, PFC, and SPC?

    • Company, troop, battery, and separate detachment CDRs.

  • 10. Para 1-9 What must be done to promote attached personnel?

    • Attached personnel may be promoted with the concurrence of the parent unit.
